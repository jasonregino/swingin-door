A San Mateo landmark

Pouring pints since 1955

Over the years the bar has seen a bit of everything. Bing Crosby would quietly sip a beer at the end of the bar, Ronald Reagan once bought the room a round, and a young Kingston Trio played the upstairs dance floor in 1957. Today Warren Chapman keeps the British tradition going, right down to the fish and chips wrapped in newspaper.
